Melisa MX
Objective of the project
  • Resolve patterns, applications or flows of development that are usually needed in web development projects.
  • Provide a highly decoupled solution scalable in terms of its modification and added functionality.
  • Provide a solid foundation for creating applications web in less time, without repeating coding (DRY).
